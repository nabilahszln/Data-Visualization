STORYTELLING&DATA VISUALIZATION INDIVIDUAL PROJECT: STATISTICS OF BURSA MALAYSIA (2015-2019)

The individual project involves analyzing data from the main dashboard of Bursa Malaysia, covering industry, audit, employment, market, and stock statistics for the years 2015 to 2019. The goal is to provide insights and trends within these sectors over the specified period, helping stakeholders understand the performance and dynamics of Malaysia's financial markets during those years using Tableau.
